## 6. Internal Linking Strategy

### Hub-and-Spoke Model
```
/london-hotels (hub)
  ├── /blog/best-luxury-hotels-london (spoke)
  ├── /blog/arab-friendly-hotels-london (spoke)
  ├── /blog/london-spa-hotels (spoke)
  └── /london-hotels/mayfair (programmatic spoke)

/london-restaurants (hub)
  ├── /blog/best-restaurants-london (spoke)
  ├── /blog/halal-restaurants-london (spoke)
  └── /london-restaurants/halal (programmatic spoke)
```

### Linking Rules
- Every blog post links to its hub page (3+ internal links minimum)
- Hub pages link to top 5-10 spoke articles
- Cross-link between related hubs (hotels ↔ restaurants ↔ experiences)
- Comparison articles link to both compared entities
